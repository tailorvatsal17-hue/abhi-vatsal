# SERVICE BOOKING SYSTEM - COMPLETE PROJECT GUIDE

This guide explains how to run, test, and manage your project.

---

## 1. HOW TO START THE PROJECT
Open your terminal in the project folder and run:
   docker-compose down -v
   docker-compose up --build

Wait until you see "Server running at http://127.0.0.1:3000/".

---

- Admin Dashboard:  http://localhost:3000/admin/index.html

## 4. HOW TO HANDLE THE OTP (OTP PROCESS)
When a customer signs up, the website will ask for an OTP. 
Since this is a test environment, you can get the OTP directly from the database:

1. Open phpMyAdmin: http://localhost:8082
2. Click on database: 'service_booking'
3. Click on table: 'otps'
4. Find the email you used and copy the 6-digit 'otp' code.
5. Paste that code into the website's OTP verification page.

---

## 5. COMPLETE WORKFLOW (Step-by-Step)

Step 1: ADD SERVICES
   - Login as ADMIN (admin@gmail.com / admin).
   - Go to 'Services' and add (e.g., Cleaning, Plumbing, Electrician).
   - Partners cannot sign up unless a service already exists.

Step 2: PARTNER SIGNUP
   - Go to the Partner Signup page (http://localhost:3000/partner/signup.html).
   - Fill out the form and select a service.
   - NOTE: The partner cannot login yet! They are "Pending".

Step 3: APPROVE PARTNER
   - Login as ADMIN again.
   - Go to 'Partners' section.
   - Click "Approve" next to the new partner.

Step 4: CUSTOMER SIGNUP & BOOKING
   - Go to Customer Signup (http://localhost:3000/signup.html).
   - Verify the OTP (see Section 4 above).
   - Login as Customer and you can now book the Approved Partner.
